Tekken 7

PC, PS5, XSX, 3+
8.2

The Ultimate Version of this Game is Almost Perfect

Todd Lewis • June 29, 2024

If you're a Tekken fan, you know how great this game is based on its longevity, popularity, and legacy.

If you're a normie, this is the greatest 3D 2D Fighter you will ever play. The amount of freedom there is in chaining combos once you learn to juggle your opponent is liberating.

BIG NEGATIVE: The game is hard. It doesn't matter what anybody says, Tekken is a hard fighting game because of the amount of functions actively firing throughout gameplay. Some characters have 200 moves. Compare that to the typical 12 of Street Fighter or 30ish of super smash characters. A BIG POSITIVE is that this is easily the most accessible of all the Tekkens thus far.
